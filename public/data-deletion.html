Data Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Data from SocialPull

SocialPull is designed with privacy in mind. We do not store your personal data or social media content on our servers. However, you can take the following steps to ensure all data is removed:

1. Remove App Access from Facebook

This is the most important step to revoke all access:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find "SocialPull" in your active apps list
  3. Click "Remove" or "Revoke Access"
  4. Confirm the removal

This immediately revokes SocialPull's access to your Facebook data and prevents any future data access.

2. Clear Local Browser Data

SocialPull stores your access token locally in your browser. To clear this:

Option A: Use SocialPull's Logout

  1. Visit SocialPull
  2. If logged in, click the "Logout" button
  3. This clears all stored tokens and data

Option B: Clear Browser Storage Manually

  1. Go to your browser settings
  2. Find "Privacy" or "Clear browsing data"
  3. Select "Cookies and site data" or "Local storage"
  4. Clear data for socialpull.netlify.app

3. What Data We Actually Store

Important: SocialPull does not store any of your social media data on our servers. Here's what happens to your data:

  • Facebook/Instagram Data: Never stored on our servers - only displayed in real-time
  • Access Tokens: Stored only in your browser's local storage
  • User Profile: Only your name and Facebook ID, stored locally in browser
  • Server Logs: May contain basic access logs (IP addresses, timestamps) - automatically purged after 30 days

4. Request Manual Data Deletion

If you want to ensure complete data removal or have concerns about your data, you can contact us:

Contact Methods:

  • Create an issue on our GitHub repository
  • Include your Facebook user ID and request type
  • We will respond within 7 business days

5. Data Deletion Confirmation

After following the steps above:

  • SocialPull will no longer have access to your Facebook data
  • No new data can be collected
  • Any locally stored tokens will be cleared
  • You can verify removal by checking Facebook's Apps and Websites settings

✅ Verification

To verify your data has been removed:

  1. Check that SocialPull is no longer listed in your Facebook Apps
  2. Visit SocialPull - you should see the login screen (not your data)
  3. Clear your browser cache if you still see old data

Questions or Issues?

If you have any questions about data deletion or need assistance:

  • Review our Privacy Policy
  • Contact us via GitHub Issues
  • We are committed to respecting your privacy and data rights
