Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

SocialPull collects and processes the following information when you use our service:

  • Facebook Profile Information: Your name and Facebook user ID for authentication
  • Page Data: Information about Facebook pages you manage, including page names, IDs, and fan counts
  • Content Data: Comments, posts, and engagement data from your Facebook pages
  • Instagram Business Data: If connected, comments and posts from Instagram business accounts linked to your pages

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display your page comments and engagement data in an organized interface
  • Authenticate your access to the service
  • Provide analytics and insights about your social media engagement

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data security is important to us:

  • We store your access token locally in your browser for session management
  • We do not store your Facebook or Instagram data on our servers
  • All data is fetched directly from Facebook's API in real-time
  • We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information or social media data to third parties. Your data is used solely for providing the SocialPull service to you.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your Facebook data at any time through Facebook's app settings
  • Clear your stored access token by logging out of the application
  • Request deletion of any data we may have collected

6. Facebook Platform Policy Compliance

SocialPull complies with Facebook's Platform Policy and terms. We:

  • Only request necessary permissions for our service functionality
  • Do not post content on your behalf
  • Respect Facebook's data use restrictions
  • Follow Facebook's guidelines for user data protection

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our GitHub repository or via the contact information provided in our application.
