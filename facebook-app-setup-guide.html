Facebook App Configuration Guide

Current Error:

The domain of this URL isn't included in the app's domains.

Step 1: Open Facebook Developer Console

  1. Go to Facebook for Developers →
  2. Find your app with ID: 1068046521647182
  3. Click on the app to open its dashboard

Step 2: Configure App Domains

  1. In the left sidebar, click "App Settings" → "Basic"
  2. Scroll down to "App Domains"
  3. Add these domains (one per line):
    localhost
    127.0.0.1
    your-future-netlify-domain.netlify.app
  4. Click "Save Changes"

Step 3: Configure Facebook Login

  1. In the left sidebar, find "Products" section
  2. If "Facebook Login" isn't added, click "+ Add Product" and add it
  3. Click "Facebook Login" → "Settings"
  4. In "Valid OAuth Redirect URIs", add:
    http://localhost:8080/
    http://127.0.0.1:8080/
    https://your-future-netlify-domain.netlify.app/
    https://your-future-netlify-domain.netlify.app/.netlify/functions/facebook-auth
  5. Set "Client OAuth Login" to "Yes"
  6. Set "Web OAuth Login" to "Yes"
  7. Click "Save Changes"
